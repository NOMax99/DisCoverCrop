About

DisCoverCrop is an interdisciplinary research project focused on cover crops, soil ecology, and sustainable farming systems. OurMotivation

Intensive agriculture has led to losses in soil fertility and biodiversity. DisCoverCrop investigates how cover crops and diversified crop rotations can reverse these trends.

Methodology

  • Field trials at multiple locations
  • Comparison of different cover crop mixtures
  • Soil analysis, yield measurements, and biodiversity monitoring
